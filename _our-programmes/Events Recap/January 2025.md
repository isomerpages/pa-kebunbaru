<h2><strong>A Dazzling Start to 2025</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Countdown_2025_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Kebun Baru was proud to lead the Ang Mo Kio GRC, Kebun Baru and Yio Chu
Kang SMCs Countdown Party 2025, held at the hardcourt in front of Mayflower
Market and Food Centre. This vibrant celebration brought our community
together for an evening of fun, music, and festive cheer.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Countdown_2025_3.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Countdown_2025_5.jpg">
</div>
<p>The night featured an exciting lineup of live music performances that
had the crowd singing and dancing along. Carnival games added to the festive
atmosphere, offering something for residents of all ages to enjoy.&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Countdown_2025_2.jpg">
</div>
<p>The highlight of the evening was the breathtaking five-minute fireworks
display at midnight, lighting up the Kebun Baru skyline and marking the
start of 2025 with a burst of colour and joy.</p>
