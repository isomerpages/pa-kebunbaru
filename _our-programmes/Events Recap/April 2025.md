<h2>Kebun Baru Sunday Carnival</h2>
<p></p>
<p>Over 4,000 residents gathered at Thomson Green Playground on 6 April 2025,
for a vibrant day filled with fun, food, and heartfelt community spirit.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/RTPS_0313__1_.jpg">
</div>
<p>We were honoured to welcome <strong>Senior Minister Lee Hsien Loong</strong>,
Adviser to Ang Mo Kio GRC GROs, and <strong>Mr Kwek Hian Chuan Henry</strong>,
Adviser to Kebun Baru GROs, as we came together to celebrate the strong
bonds that make our community so special.</p>
<p>A special highlight of the day was a moving performance by <strong>Daniel Ng</strong>,
a visually impaired performer whose inspiring talent touched many hearts.</p>
<p>The event also marked the launch of our exciting new partnership with
the <strong>NUS Academy for Healthy Longevity</strong>, reinforcing our
commitment to supporting active and healthy ageing in Kebun Baru.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/DSC_7885__1_.jpg">
</div>
<p>Residents were also delighted by the presence of familiar faces from the
entertainment scene, including <strong>Lee Nanxing, Vivian Lai, Constance Song</strong>,
and our energetic emcee <strong>Allan Wu</strong>, who added star power
and cheer to the festivities.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/DSC_7938__1_.jpg">
</div>
<p>From carnival games and delicious local fare, to a pony workshop for kids
and a dog corner for pet lovers, there was truly something for everyone.</p>
