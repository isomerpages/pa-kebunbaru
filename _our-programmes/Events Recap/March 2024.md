<h3><strong>Ang Mo Kio 1 CRRT Year 2 TTX</strong></h3>
<p>Over a 100 grassroots and community partners came together on 10 Mar 2024
to discuss the shared responsibility of community leaders in crisis prevention.
The tabletalk exercise brought stakeholders into the fold to become the
platform for organic grassroots initiatives that can bolster community
readiness and resilience across various sectors.</p>
