<h2>Kebun Baru Post Budget Dialogue 2025</h2>
<p></p>
<p>On 30 March 2025, residents of Kebun Baru came together for the annual
Post-Budget Dialogue, a meaningful platform to discuss Budget 2025 and
its impact on the community. During the session, residents had the opportunity
to engage directly with Adviser to Kebun Baru Grassroots Organisations,
Mr Kwek Hian Chuan Henry, raising questions and sharing feedback on various
budget measures. The dialogue covered key areas such as support for families,
seniors, businesses, and efforts to build a more sustainable and inclusive
Singapore.</p>
