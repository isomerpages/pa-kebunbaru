<h2>Kebun Baru Edusave Awards Ceremony</h2>
<p>On 22 February 2025, the Kebun Baru Edusave Awards Ceremony was held to
celebrate the achievements of our young residents. Students who excelled
academically were honoured with Edusave Awards, in recognition of their
hard work, perseverance, and commitment to learning. The event brought
together families, educators, and community members in a shared moment
of pride and encouragement, as we continue to support and uplift the next
generation in their educational journey.</p>

<h2>Chinese New Year Dinner 2025</h2>
<p>On 9th February 2025, Day 12 of the Chinese New Year, Kebun Baru hosted
its annual Chinese New Year Dinner, and we were thrilled to see 1,000 attendees
join us in celebration.</p>
<p></p>

PCF Kids for their incredible performance, and our very own Chinese Orchestra
and Ulan LAN SekumpULAN, the Dikir Barat ensemble, and Taijiquan for their
