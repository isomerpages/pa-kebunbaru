<h3><strong>Community Repair Workshop</strong></h3>
<p>A group of handy and committed volunteers have come together to teach
residents how to repair their faulty items. Residents get to learn new
skills and repair their electrical appliances at this monthly free workshop.
Join the next session with something you would like to fix or just to observe
and pick up a new skill!</p>
