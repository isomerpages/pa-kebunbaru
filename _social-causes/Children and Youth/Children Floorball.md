<h3><strong>Children's Floorball</strong></h3>
<p>Hosted by Kebun Baru Community Sports Network (CSN), there has been a
floorball programme for children in Kebun Baru from less privileged backgrounds
for the past few years. They are mentored and have the opportunity to represent
Kebun Baru in floorball tournaments such as the Singapore Community Games.</p>
